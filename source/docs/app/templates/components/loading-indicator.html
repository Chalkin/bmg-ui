Loading Indicator

Waiting animation for content that is currently loading. 

<div class="content-loading">
    <i class="fa fa-spin fa-spinner content-placeholder-loading"></i>
</div>

You can also specify a function that loads the content. Until the promise returned by that function resolves, the loading animation will be displayed: 

<data-content-placeholder
    data-content-promise="ctrl.loadContent()">
    <h2 data-ng-bind="ctrl.content.heading"></h2>
    <p data-ng-bind="ctrl.content.text"></p>
</data-content-placeholder>

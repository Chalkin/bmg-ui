Notifications

Take a look into the navbar for the notification icon and the notification count.

<notification
	title="ctrl.notifications.title"
	template="ctrl.notifications.template"
	data="ctrl.notifications.data"
	handle-url="ctrl.notifications.handleUrl({notification: notification})"
	handle-highlighted-url="ctrl.notifications.handleHighlightedUrl({notification: notification})"
	mark-all-as-read="ctrl.notifications.markAllAsRead()"
	load-more="ctrl.notifications.loadMoreItems()"></notification>
Dependencies: angular-notification-icons, ng-infinite-scroll, angular-moment, angular-animate
